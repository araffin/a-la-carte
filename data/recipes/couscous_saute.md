## Couscous saut√© roquette 

### Ingredients

For 2 people:

- ~50g rocket (around half a packet)
- 2 eggs
- 1 onion
- a bit cheese
- couscous
- tomato (~20 cherry tomatoes are even better)
- coconut oil
- [Optional] mint

### Steps

1. Prepare the couscous (with a bit of olive oil/ghee/seasoning (salt+pepper)/*harissa*)
2. Cook the onion in coconut oil (you can add also a bit of turmeric/jeera powder)
3. Add some rocket (keep some fresh rocket for the end)
4. Add some tomatoes cut in small (keep also some aside to add at the end)
5. Start cooking the egg
6. Add couscous (and a bit of ghee) and some cheese cut in small 
7. Before serving put a bit of fresh rocket, fresh tomato and mint on top
