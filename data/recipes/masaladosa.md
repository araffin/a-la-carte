## Masala Dosa



### Ingredients

For 2 people:

#### Potato palya

- 3 medium sized potatoes boiled
- ½ medium red onion sliced
- ½ inch ginger chopped
- 1 green chilli slit
- ½ tsp asafoetida
- pinch of ajwain seeds
- pinch of cumin seeds

#### Dosa

Mix dosa batter with water and salt, adjust until you get the desired consistency.

### Steps

1. Boil potatoes with some salt
2. Heat some oil and splutter mustard seeds and ajwain when hot
3. Saute ginger, onion and the green chilli for a few minutes 
4. Add salt, asafoetida and turmeric
5. Add the mixture to mashed boiled potatoes
