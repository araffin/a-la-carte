## Quiche



### Ingredients

For 4 people:

- 1 pâte brisée (see [dough recipe of tarte à la tomate](./tartelatomate.html))
- mushrooms (~600g)
- 1 pod garlic
- herbs (sage)
- 4 tbsp crème fraîche
- 15cl milk (can be oat milk)
- 3 eggs
- ~100g grated cheese
- salt, pepper, (noix de muscade is optional)

### Steps

0. Prepare the [dough]((./tartelatomate.html))
1. Pre-bake the dough (5-10 minutes at 200°C)
2. Cook the mushrooms, don't forget to season with salt/pepper/sage
3. Mix the cream with eggs and milk, add the cheese
4. Distribute the mushrooms, poor the mix on top
5. Bake until nicely cooked (~25 minutes at 200°C)
