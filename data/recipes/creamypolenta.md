## Creamy polenta



### Ingredients

For N people:

- 500 ml water
- 1 table spoon vegetables’ broth powder
- 1 clove garlic
- 125 g polenta
- 100 ml cream 
- 5 sage leaves 
- salt & pepper

### Steps

1. Boil the water and add the vegetables’ broth powder and pressed garlic clove. 
2. Add the polenta, stir quickly to avoid lump formation and let boil shortly (attention: polenta gets messy when boiling, so cover immediately after stirring!).
3. Chop the sage leaves.
4. Take the pot out of the stove, mix in the chopped sage and let the mixture swell for 5 mins. 
5. Mix in the cream, season with salt & pepper, and serve. Enjoy!
