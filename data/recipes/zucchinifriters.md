## Zucchini friters



### Ingredients

For 2 people:

- 1 egg
- 1 zucchini 200 g
- Flour - few tbsp
- Baking powder - pinch
- Salt
- Pepper
- Onion - half
- Garlic - 1 clove
- Parsley/Mint few leaves

### Steps

1. Soak grated zucchini in some salt for 10 min.
2. Chop onion and garlic, parsley/mint
3. Toast them in some butter till light golden. Season with salt, pepper and a dash of nutmeg.
4. Squeeze out water from zucchini.Mix with egg, flour adjusting to consistency of batter, baking powder, salt and pepper.
5. Add the fried onion mixture.  Mix well.
6. Scoop a few tsp of batter into a pan with heated oil. Spread gently with the spoon to make it flat and cook covered. Flip both sides till golden brown.
