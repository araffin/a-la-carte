## Thokku



### Ingredients

For 3 people:

- 2 medium onions (~ 1/2 cup)
- 3 pods garlic
- 2 inches ginger
- 1 green chili
- 1 tsp mustard seed
- 1 tbsp curry leafs
- 2 medium size aubergine
- 2 tsp tamarind paste
- jaggery as per taste
- salt
- tumeric
- red chili powder


### Steps

#### For the aubergine:

1. Slice brinjal (aubergine) into slices and soak in turmeric water.
2. Drain the water and coat with turmeric chili powder and salt and shallow fry the individual slices till well done and keep aside.

#### For the gravy:

1. Chop one large or 2 medium onions finely.
2. Chop green chillies, ginger and garlic.
3. Heat oil in a kadai. Add mustard, methi seeds and then saut√© the onion chillies etc with curry leaves till the onions are transparent.
4. Then add a dash of turmeric and chilli powder and pour in tamarind water and cook till the raw taste is gone.
5. Add the brinjal slices and simmer on a low fire for a bit and then add a bit of jaggery
6. Finally a dash of coriander leaves and it's all done.
