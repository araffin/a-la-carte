## Marlis' Letscho

[Original recipe website](https://bbqpit.de/letscho/)

### Ingredients

For 4 people:

- 2 medium red paprika
- 1 medium yellow paprika
- 1 medium onion 
- 2 big pods garlic
- 1 cup tomato puree or equivalent fresh tomatoes
- 1 tsp paprika edelsuss
- 1 tsp paprika rosenscharf
- 1 tsp eros pista
- 1 cube vegetable broth
- 100 g tarhonya (Hungarian pasta)
- 300ml water

### Steps

1. Slice paprika, onion, tomatoes and garlic into long strips.
2. Season with two paprika powders, eros pista, salt and drizzle olive oil.
3. Preheat oven and grill the sliced vegetables in a flat tray on high heat for around 20 min
3. Boil water with vegetable broth and cook the tajona in it for around 10 min. 
4. Combine the cooked Hungarian pasta with vegetable broth and the baked vegetables in a dish with depth and bake for another 10 min in the oven.
5. Garnish with coriander or parsley.
