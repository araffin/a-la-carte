## Lotti's zucchini i pasta 



### Ingredients

For 3-4 people:

- 2 big zucchinis
- 3-4 pods of garlic
- some Italian seasoning (thyme/oregano,...)
- olive oil
- pasta (farfale like)
- canned tomatoes (not purée)
- optional: rocket


### Steps

1. slice the zucchinis, not too thin, not too fat 
2. toast a bit the Zucchini in the pan without oil
3. when it starts to release water (don't overcook!), add oil and garlic and season
4. Add the whole can tomatoes
5. add the cooked pasta and c'est fini! 
6. optional: you can put some rucola/rocket or cheese on top

Note: can be had as a salad the next day
