## Mamma’s Upmav



### Ingredients

For 1 person:

- 1 cups of rava (semolina) roasted
- 1 ½ cups of water
- ½ tsp salt

- ¼ cup onion chopped
- 1-2 green chillies chopped
- ¼ inch ginger chopped
- 1 small sprigs Curry leaves chopped
- ½ tsp mustard seeds
- 1 dry red chilli (not necessary)

### Steps

1. Heat 1 tbsp of oil and ½ tbsp ghee in a wok.
2. Splutter mustard seeds and dry chilli.
3. Toss onions, ginger, green chillies and curry leaves.
4. Add water and salt and bring to a boil.
5. Add the rava and stir continuously till slurry.
6. Cover with a lid till soft and cooked.
