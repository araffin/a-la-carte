## Riste d'aubergine



### Ingredients

- aubergine
- onion
- garlic
- tomato sauce
- paprika
- herbs

### Steps

1. Roast aubergines in oil, reserve
2. Optional, roast paprika, no oil, reserve
3. cook onion and garlic
4. and put the rest with tomato sauce, salt, pepper, spice and herbs
5. slow cook forever
6. serve with pasta
