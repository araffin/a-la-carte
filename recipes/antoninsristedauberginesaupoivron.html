Recipe | Antonin's riste d'aubergines au poivron

Antonin's riste d'aubergines au poivron

Ingredients

For ~2 people:

  • 1 aubergine
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • Tomato puree 200g
  • [optional] Oat/cashew cream
  • Thyme, organo, basil, rosemary, bay leaf, sage, paprika powder, Cayenne pepper
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Chop the veggies
  2. Cook the aubergine in oil and a bit of water and cover and toss from time to time. Cook on high flame until charred slightly.
  3. Add oil, bell pepper
  4. Add onion and garlic
  5. Add tomato puree and the spices/herbs
  6. Cook until a bit dry
  7. Add cream/water
  8. [optional] Can be served with pasta/bread
