Champignon ragout

From Maria

Ingredients

For 4 people:

  • 500 g fresh mushrooms
  • 2 small onions
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 2 bell peppers (ideally red & yellow)
  • 150 ml concentrated tomato paste
  • 4-5 tablespoons olive oil
  • bell pepper powder
  • salt & pepper
  • thyme
  • polenta (see polenta page)

Steps

  1. Clean the onion and garlic, cut into cubes and cook in olive oil for 5 minutes.
  2. Add the chopped bell peppers and mushrooms (cut into big pieces). Let them simmer covered in their own juice for 10 minutes.
  3. Then add the tomato paste, season with bell pepper powder, thyme, salt and pepper to taste and cook at low heat for 10 minutes (optional: put in the oven).
  4. Serve the ragout with the polenta and enjoy!
