Ana's guacamole

Ingredients

For 4 people:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 2 garlic pods
  • big squeeze of lemon (1/4 of green lemon)
  • salt
  • [Optional] onion
  • [Optional] jalapeno

Steps

  1. Mash avocado
  2. Grate garlic
  3. Add lemon juice and salt to taste
